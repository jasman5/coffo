NAINITAL

in Uttrakhand

Nainital, the charming Himalayan lake town, is a picture-postcard perfect hill-station and one of the most popular in Northern India.

Naini Lake

The town of Nainital is known mostly for Naini Lake, a popular Nainital tourist spot surrounded by verdant hills. Sailing, rowing,and paddling are all options for vacationers.

Mall Road

On Mall Road, you can browse among stores, large and small, selling all sorts of goods. Candle ships and candle fruits are available for purchase and can be used as home decor.
